Macan


Opportunities don't wait. So what are you waiting for? 20" Macan S wheels painted in Dark Titanium, Sport Chrono Package, and a chassis lowered by 15mm with Porsche Active Suspension Management, deliver sharper driving dynamics. Unique exterior detailing in Agate Grey Metallic is instantly recognisable.

An Exhilarating Drive. Unique exterior details in Agate Grey metallic. A striking detail on the front end: the prominent spoiler with an emphasis on width. Painted in Agate Grey metallic to match the sideblades, exterior mirrors, roof spoiler and lettering on the rear.

LED main headlights including PDLS. LED main headlights with Porsche Dynamic Light System (PDLS) feature static and dynamic cornering lights. The distinctive four-point daytime running light signature is unmistakably Porsche.

Front sports seats (8-way). The electric front sport seats with 8-way adjustment provide a perfect driving position and feature Sport-Tex striped seat centres in silver, and Porsche Crests on the headrests.
